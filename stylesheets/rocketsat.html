ROCKET SAT

- University of Northern Colorado -

Rocket SAT is a program funded by the Colorado Space Grant Consortium for students to design and build a sounding rocket payload and launch the payload on a rocket out of Wallops Flight Facility. Payloads are student-built and reviewed extensively by NASA engieers to provide rigorous aerospace engineering experience to undergraduate students.

There are two variants of Rocket SAT:

  • "Rocket SAT - C" - Sounding rocket with a pressurized payload section
  • "Rocket SAT - X" - Sounding rocket with an unpressurized payload section with ferring seperation at apogee
